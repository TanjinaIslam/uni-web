Offered Degrees

Undergraduate Program

The education system in Bangladesh encompasses various bachelor's
degree programmes with a nominal duration varying from 3 to 5 years.

Postgraduate Program

It generally takes 1-2 years for completing a Master's degree in Bangladesh.
Earning an advanced degree, such as a Master offers the ability to increase your skillset
and possibly improve your employment chances at the same time.
